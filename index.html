Welcome to Kiama Green Acres

Nurturing the earth,feeding families

Welcome to Kiama Green Acres, nestled at the foot of Karima Hill in Othaya. Our farm thrives with fresh produce, from vibrant capsicums to hearty cabbages. A blend of tradition and innovation, we combine dairy and fish farming, offering fresh milk, dairy products, and sustainable fish. We prioritize environmental well-being and local food security while respecting the land. Visit us to experience the vibrant spirit of rural Kenya, where every product reflects our commitment to quality and sustainability
